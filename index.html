About Me

I am passionate about crafting intuitive and engaging user experiences. As an international student, I bring a unique perspective to my design work, enriched by a diverse cultural background. My journey in UX/UI design is fueled by a desire to bridge the gap between users and technology,creating seamless and enjoyable digital experiences. I have honed my skills through various projects, including leading the development of the "ExploreEase" app, which helps users find affordable housing, dining options, and recreational activities in new environments. I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to innovative design projects and continue growing as a UX/UI designer. My goal is to create user-centric designs that not only meet but exceed user expectations, ensuring that technology remains accessible and enjoyable for all.

Portfolio

XPLOREEASE APP

XploreEase is an app designed to assist newcomers in navigating their new environments by helping them find affordable housing, high-quality dining options, and engaging recreational activities. The app aims to make the transition smoother for people moving to a new area by providing them with valuable information and resources to settle in comfortably.

SKILLSWAP

SkillSwap is an app designed to connect individuals who want to learn new skills with those who have expertise in those areas. It facilitates a mutually beneficial exchange where users can offer their skills in return for learning something new. For example, someone proficient in graphic design might teach another user, while in exchange, they learn a new language or cooking technique. The app aims to foster a community of continuous learning and skill-sharing, making it easy for users to enhance their abilities and knowledge through direct, peer-to-peer interaction.

Xylophone Project

For the Xylophone project, I developed a music app using Xcode that significantly deepened my understanding of Apple documentation, Swift functions, and the process of linking multiple elements to a single IBAction. This project allowed me to integrate various components seamlessly, enhancing my skills in app development and reinforcing best practices in coding and UI design.
